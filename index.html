Who I Am

I am a budding web developer living in Durham, NC. I love vivid colors, whimsy and little surprises where you wouldn't expect them. I have fun experimenting and exploring. My goal is to bring all of these things to well thought out project for my clients that is not only easy to use, but pleasureable.

When I'm not staring at a screen (laptop, phone, netflix or otherwise) I'm downtown getting ice cream, at Eno Park wading in the river or enjoy the art scene in my wonderful city. Preferably with my family and/or friends.
